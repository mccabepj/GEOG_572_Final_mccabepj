ESRI 'Map Tour':
H Street Marathon

This project was an opportunity to experiment with and explore the development of a map for other users to use. Under the assignment criteria, I decided that the Streetcar running along H St NE in DC would be a fun setting. While developing the map tour concept, I also realized that it was 13 blocks in each direction, and thought it was appropriate to consider this a 'marathon' of some sort.
